The Cvent Supplier Network
A Free Online Sourcing Tool for Meeting Planners

The Supplier Network brings together planners who are looking to source their events and meetings with venues seeking to book their meeting space and sleep rooms. Planners can search for venues and services, compare property and company details and find exclusive promotions. Planners send eRFPs through the Supplier Network to selected venues and service providers. Suppliers can then easily respond to RFPs by submitting their proposals through the system.

Planners benefit from the efficiency and wealth of data; venues are able to market their properties in front of thousands of qualified planners.
